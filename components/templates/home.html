Welcome to TrendyTrader! This is our capstone project we have created for our Data Science 440 course. For this project we were able to develop a a tool for stock market analysis. Through gathering data, we scraped from a Twitter API, we have been able to conduct sentiment analysis on numerous tweets and use that information from the sentiment analysis to give each tweet a score. On TrendyTrader users can review specific stocks they want to learn more about.


TrendyTrader is more than a project to us, it has been a semester long effort that has challenged us as students and allowed us to showcase some of the skills we have been able to develop at data science students in our four years the Pennsylvania State University.
